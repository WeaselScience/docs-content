<Macro id="iam-requirements" />

<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway Console](https://console.scaleway.com/)
  - You have [created](/storage/block/how-to/create-a-volume/) a Block Storage volume
</Message>

<Message type="note">
  The only way for you to delete a Block volume, is if it is detached from the Instance. Refer to our documenation to find out [how to detach a Block volume from an Instance](/storage/block/how-to/detach-a-volume/).
</Message>

1. Click **Block Storage** in the **Storage** section of the Scaleway Console side menu. A list of your volumes displays.
2. Click <Icon name="more" /> > **Delete** next to the volume of your choice. A pop-up displays.
5. Click **Delete**. A pop-up displays.
6. Type `DELETE` to confirm.
7. Click **Delete volume** to remove the volume from your account.
    <Message type="important">
      This action permanently deletes your volume and all the data it contains. If you do not want to lose this data, make sure you back your data before deleting the volume.
    </Message>
