You can detach a Block Storage volume from your Instance at any time. It is important to [unmount (detach) the device](/storage/block/how-to/unmount-a-volume/) from the operating system before detaching the Block Storage volume to avoid data loss.

<Macro id="iam-requirements" />

<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com)
  - You have created a [Block Storage volume](/storage/block/how-to/create-a-volume)
  - You have a [Block Storage volume](/storage/block/how-to/create-a-volume) attached to an Instance
</Message>

1. Click **Block Storage** in the **Storage** section of the Scaleway Console side menu. The Block Storage page displays.
2. Click <Icon name="more" /> > **Detach from Instance** next to the volume of your choice. A pop-up displays.
3. Click **Detach this volume** to confirm.
