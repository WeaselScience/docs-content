## How to attach a volume
1. Click **Instances** from the left side menu. The Instances section displays.
2. Click **Volumes** to display a list of all your current volumes. The newly created volume displays on top of the list:

    <Lightbox src="scaleway-volume-created.webp" alt="" />
3. Click **Attach to instance** to choose the instance to which the volume shall be attached.

## How to increase the volume size
1. Click the **Volumes** tab on the Instance information page of the console. A list of your volumes displays.
    <Message type="tip">
      You must go to the [Volumes tab](https://console.scaleway.com/instance/volumes) in the Scaleway console and select the volume corresponding to the Instance you want to increase.
    </Message>
2. Click <Icon name="more" /> beside the volume you want to increase and then click **Increase Volume**.
3. Increase the size according to your preference with the <Icon name="plus" /> button.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-increase-volume-modal.webp" alt="" />
4. Click **Increase volume** to confirm.
    <Message type="note">
      Currently, only the **increase** of a Block Storage volume is supported. The maximum size of a Block volume is 10 TB.
    </Message>


## How to detach a volume
You can detach a Block Storage volume from your Instance at any time. It is important to [unmount (detach) the device](/storage/block/how-to/unmount-a-volume/) from the operating system before detaching the Block Storage volume to avoid data loss.

<Macro id="iam-requirements" />

<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com)
  - You have a [Block Storage volume](/storage/block/concepts/#block-device) attached to a Scaleway Instance
</Message>

1. Click **Instances** in the **Compute** section of the [Scaleway Console](https://console.scaleway.com) side menu. A list of your Instances displays.
2. Click the Instance with the volume you want to detach. The Instance information page displays.
3. Click **Attached Volumes**. A list of all volumes attached to the Instance displays.
4. Click <Icon name="more" /> next to the volume you want to detach and then on **Detach**. A pop-up displays.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-detach-volume.webp" alt="" />
5. Click **Detach volume** to confirm.

## How to delete a volume
<Message type="note">
  The only way for you to delete a Block volume, is if it is detached from the Instance. Refer to our documenation to find out [how to detach a Block volume from an Instance](/storage/block/how-to/detach-a-volume/).
</Message>

1. Click **Instances** in the **Compute** section of the [Scaleway Console](https://console.scaleway.com) side menu. A list of your Instances displays.
3. Click the **Volumes** tab to view a list of your volumes.
4. Click <Icon name="more" /> next to the volume that you want to delete.
5. Click **Delete**. A pop-up displays.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-block-delete-volume.webp" alt="" />
6. Type `DELETE` to confirm.
7. Click **Delete volume** to remove the volume from your account.
    <Message type="important">
      All data on the volume will be removed when you delete it. Make sure you have a backup of your data before removing the volume.
    </Message>
